Who we are

A generation that gives back

Crumble for Kindness is a youth-led nonprofit based in the Lower Mainland, British Columbia. We believe young people have the power to create real, lasting change in their communities. Our mission is to mobilize students and youth to raise funds and awareness for BC Children's Hospital.

What we do

How we make an impact

Fundraising Events

Community and school-based events across the Lower Mainland that bring youth together for a great cause.

School Chapters

Youth chapters across Lower Mainland schools working together to raise funds and awareness.

BC Children's Hospital

Every dollar raised goes directly to support kids and families at BCCH.

Youth Leadership

Building the next generation of compassionate, community-minded leaders across British Columbia.
